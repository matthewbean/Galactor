# Galactor
Galactor is an exciting arcade-style game that challenges players to survive multiple waves of enemies with increasing difficulty. Armed with powerful weapons that can be upgraded throughout the game, players must blast their way through hordes of alien ships, robots, and other hostile creatures. With each wave, the enemies become stronger and more numerous, requiring players to use all their skills to avoid getting overwhelmed. Fast-paced and action-packed, Galactor is a perfect game for players looking for a quick, adrenaline-pumping experience that they can enjoy in short bursts. So, grab your weapons, power up your ship, and get ready for an epic battle in Galactor!
